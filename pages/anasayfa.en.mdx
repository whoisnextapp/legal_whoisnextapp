# Whoisnextapp — Legal Documentation

**WIN** is a secure and transparent social matching platform that places user safety and data protection standards at its core. This portal provides all **published legal documentation** for the WIN application and related services in one place: agreements, privacy and data protection texts, community guidelines, corporate compliance pages, and application forms.

---

## What's in this portal?

Documents are organized under **four main categories** and **application forms**. Navigate directly from the left menu or use the cards below.

---

## How to use this portal?

- **Left menu:** All categories and page titles are listed; click a title to navigate to the document.
- **Search:** Use the search box in the top right to search by agreement name or clause content.
- **External links:** The menu includes WIN's official website (whoisnextapp.com), App Store and Google Play pages, and a contact link.
