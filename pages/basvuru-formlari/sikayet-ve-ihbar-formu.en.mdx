# Complaint and Reporting Form

This form is used for formally submitting complaints/reports regarding **user behaviors** or **content** that violate community rules or general policies, within the scope of the [Community Guidelines](/topluluk-ve-guvenlik/topluluk-kurallari) and [Terms of Use](/sozlesmeler/kullanim-kosullari) (Section 7).

This form is completed by **private individuals** (users or third parties). The primary method is the in-app **"Report / Notify"** button; this form is preferred when a written record or additional documentation is required. **Official authorities** must use the [Illegal Content Notification Form](/basvuru-formlari/yasa-disi-icerik-bildirim-formu) for illegal content notifications.

## Download the form

After filling out and signing the form, you can scan it and send it to **[support@whoisnextapp.com](mailto:support@whoisnextapp.com)**.

## How to fill it out?

### Purpose of the form

This form is for making a formal complaint/report about **user behaviors** or **content** that violate community rules or general policies. Priority is in-app "**Report / Notify**"; this form is used when a written record or additional documentation is required. For content-only moderation notifications, the [Content Violation Form](/basvuru-formlari/icerik-ihlal-formu) may also be used. For official authorities, the [Illegal Content Notification Form](/basvuru-formlari/yasa-disi-icerik-bildirim-formu) applies.

### Fields to be completed

| Section                                               | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| ----------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **1. Information of complainant / reporting person**  | Name, surname, and email (required); phone is optional. If you are a WIN member, user ID or registered email speeds up identity matching.                                                                                                          |
| **2. Report / complaint type**                        | Mark relevant categories: harassment/bullying, threat/blackmail, non-consensual sexual content, fraud, fake account, suspicion of minor, personal data exposure/doxxing, hate speech, threat of violence, spam, rating abuse, other.             |
| **3. References of the complained user / content**    | Profile or screen name; WIN user ID (if any); content location (profile, message, photo; date/time); incident/interaction date range. Under DSA, the "exact location" of the content is required.                                                 |
| **4. Reasoned explanation**                           | Briefly explain what happened and why it is contrary to the Community Guidelines or Terms of Use (minimum element including DSA).                                                                                                                     |
| **5. Additional evidence or explanation**             | Screenshot, message summary, date/time note; send additional files as email attachments, indicate in the form as "Attachment: ...".                                                                                                                  |
| **6. Good faith and accuracy statement**              | Confirm that you believe the information in the notice is accurate and complete, and that you submit in good faith. False or bad-faith notices may be subject to sanctions.                                                                          |
| **7. Date and signature**                             | Write the date as day/month/year and sign. Electronic submission is sufficient; wet signature is requested only if specifically required.                                                                                                             |

### Documents to attach

- **Evidence:** Screenshots or message summaries may be sent as email attachments; include a note in the form such as "Attachment: 2 screenshots added".
- No mandatory additional document is required.

### Submission and important notes

- **Submission:** Scan the form and send it by email to **[support@whoisnextapp.com](mailto:support@whoisnextapp.com)**.
- After reporting, it is recommended that you **block** the relevant user.
- Reports are evaluated within the framework of the Community Guidelines and Terms of Use; actions are taken under Law No. 5651 and DSA.
