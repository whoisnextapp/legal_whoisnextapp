# Illegal Content Notification Form

This form is used for notifications of content that clearly constitutes a crime, by **official authorities and authorized institutions**, pursuant to Law No. 5651 and relevant legal regulations (DSA, etc.).

**Private individuals** (users, lawyers, companies) do not use this form; they should use the [Complaint and Reporting Form](/basvuru-formlari/sikayet-ve-ihbar-formu) or the in-app "Report / Notify" feature.

---

After filling out and signing the form, you can scan it and send it to **[support@whoisnextapp.com](mailto:support@whoisnextapp.com)**.

## How to fill it out?

### Purpose of the form

This form is used **only by official authorities and authorized institutions** (law enforcement, prosecutor's office, court, administrative authority, etc.). Private individuals should use the [Complaint and Reporting Form](/basvuru-formlari/sikayet-ve-ihbar-formu) or the in-app "Report / Notify" feature. Request format and processes are aligned with the [Law Enforcement Guide](/kurumsal-ve-yasal-uyumluluk/kolluk-kuvvetleri-rehberi) and the [DSA Compliance Page](/kurumsal-ve-yasal-uyumluluk/dsa-uyum-sayfasi).

### Fields to be completed

| Section                                          | Description                                                                                                                                       |
| ------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **1. Information of the notifying authority / authorized person** | Institution, unit, requester name-title, file/document number, contact information (email, phone, address).                                     |
| **2. Legal basis and request type**              | Citation of law/article (5651, TCC, DSA, etc.); request type: content removal, access blocking, information request, data preservation, or emergency. |
| **3. Legal nature of the content and offense type** | Why the content is unlawful; relevant TCC/5651/DSA provision; brief reasoned explanation.                                                        |
| **4. Content reference and access information**  | User ID, profile name, email/phone (if any); content ID; content location and date range; screenshot or link where possible.                    |
| **5. Request summary and additional requests**   | A clear summary of what action you request.                                                                                                      |
| **6. Confidentiality / notification restriction** | If user notification must not be made, mark this and state the reason.                                                                           |
| **7. Good faith and accuracy statement**         | Statement that the information is accurate and complete, and submitted in official authority capacity.                                            |
| **8. Date and signature / seal**                 | Must be completed with official letter or wet signature.                                                                                          |

### Documents to attach

- **Official document:** If the request is made by official letter, the relevant letter or decision copy may be sent as an email attachment.
- **Evidence:** Screenshot or access information related to the content should be attached where possible.

### Submission and important notes

- **Submission:** You can send the form (and official letter, if any) by email to **[support@whoisnextapp.com](mailto:support@whoisnextapp.com)**. Emergency or duly official channels are explained in the [Law Enforcement Guide](/kurumsal-ve-yasal-uyumluluk/kolluk-kuvvetleri-rehberi).
- For detailed request format and processes, review the [Law Enforcement Guide](/kurumsal-ve-yasal-uyumluluk/kolluk-kuvvetleri-rehberi) and the [DSA Compliance Page](/kurumsal-ve-yasal-uyumluluk/dsa-uyum-sayfasi).
