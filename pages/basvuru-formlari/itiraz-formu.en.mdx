## How to fill it out?

### Purpose of the form

This form is intended for submitting an official appeal against **moderation decisions** (content removal, account suspension or closure, feature restriction) under [Terms of Use](/sozlesmeler/kullanim-kosullari) Section 7 and [Community Guidelines](/topluluk-ve-guvenlik/topluluk-kurallari). You must be the addressee of the decision; if you are a legal representative, attach an authorization document. Priority is in-app support (Ticket) or support email; this form is used when a written and structured application is required.

### Fields to be completed

| Section                                       | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| --------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **1. Information of the appellant (applicant)** | Name, surname, and email (the address where you received the decision notice) are required; phone is optional. WIN user ID or registered email speeds up account matching.                                                                   |
| **2. Information of the appealed decision**   | Decision type (content removal, temporary suspension, permanent closure, feature restriction, other); date of decision notice and notification method; decision/reference number or email subject (if any); brief description of the appealed content (if content removal). |
| **3. Grounds for appeal (reasoned explanation)** | Briefly explain why the decision is incorrect or unfair, and which rule or fact was misassessed (minimum element including DSA).                                                                                                             |
| **4. Evidence or additional information**     | Screenshot, copy of decision notice, content ID, or date/time note; send additional files as email attachments, indicate in the form as "Attachment: ...".                                                                                  |
| **5. Good faith and accuracy statement**      | Confirm that you believe the information provided is accurate and complete, and that you submit the appeal in good faith. Intentional false information or bad-faith appeals may be subject to sanctions.                                  |
| **6. Date and signature**                     | Write the date as day/month/year and sign. Electronic submission is sufficient; wet signature is requested only if specifically required.                                                                                                    |

### Documents to attach

- **Evidence:** Decision email or screenshots may be sent as email attachments; you may briefly write in the form, "Attachment: decision email attached".
- No mandatory additional document is required.

### Submission and important notes

- **Submission:** Fill out and scan the form, then send it by email to **[support@whoisnextapp.com](mailto:support@whoisnextapp.com)**.
- **Deadline:** The appeal must be submitted **within 6 months at the latest** from the decision notice; for faster resolution, application within 30 days is recommended where possible.
- **Review:** Appeals are evaluated through human review. Details are provided in [Terms of Use](/sozlesmeler/kullanim-kosullari) Section 7 and the [DSA Compliance Page](/kurumsal-ve-yasal-uyumluluk/dsa-uyum-sayfasi).
