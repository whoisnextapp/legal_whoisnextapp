# Content Violation Form

This form is used for formally reporting **content** to the moderation team that you believe is disturbing, contrary to platform rules, or objectionable, within the scope of the [Community Guidelines](/topluluk-ve-guvenlik/topluluk-kurallari), [Terms of Use](/sozlesmeler/kullanim-kosullari) (Sections 5, 7), and the relevant legal framework (Law No. 5651, DSA, etc.).

This form is completed by **private individuals** (users or third parties). The primary method is the in-app **"Report / Notify"** button; this form is preferred when a written record or additional documentation is required. **Official authorities** must use the [Illegal Content Notification Form](/basvuru-formlari/yasa-disi-icerik-bildirim-formu) for illegal content notifications. For copyright/trademark violations, use the [Intellectual Property (Copyright) Infringement Form](/basvuru-formlari/fikri-mulkiyet-telif-ihlal-formu).

## How to fill it out?

### Purpose of the form

This form is for **content** violation reports (moderation) against platform rules. It is used to report behavior/content considered a violation of the Community Guidelines or Terms of Use. Priority is the in-app **"Report / Notify"** option; this form is preferred when a written record or additional documentation is required. For official authorities or copyright/trademark violations, the relevant other forms must be used.

### Fields to be completed

| Section                                       | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| --------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **1. Information of the reporting person**    | Name, surname, and email (required); phone is optional. If you are a WIN member, user ID or registered email speeds up identity matching.                                                                                                                                        |
| **2. Description and location of the violating content** | Content type (profile text, photo, message, etc.); user who shared the content; WIN user ID (if any); content location and date/time; incident/access date range. Missing location information may extend review time.                                                            |
| **3. Violation type**                         | Mark all items you consider relevant from categories aligned with the Community Guidelines: non-consensual sexual/obscene, harassment, threat, hate speech, obscene/nudity, suspicion of minor, fake account, doxxing, fraud, spam, rating abuse, other.                         |
| **4. Reasoned explanation**                   | Briefly explain what the content is and why it is considered a rule violation (minimum element including DSA).                                                                                                                                                                     |
| **5. Evidence or additional information**     | Screenshot, message summary, date/time note; send additional files as email attachments, indicate in the form as "Attachment: ...".                                                                                                                                               |
| **6. Good faith and accuracy statement**      | Confirm that you believe the information in the report is accurate and complete, and that you submit in good faith. False or bad-faith reports may be subject to sanctions.                                                                                                       |
| **7. Date and signature**                     | Write the date as day/month/year and sign. Electronic submission is sufficient; wet signature is requested only if specifically required.                                                                                                                                          |

### Documents to attach

- **Evidence:** Screenshots or message text summaries may be sent as email attachments; include a short note in the form such as "Attachment: 2 screenshots added".
- No mandatory additional document is required; minimum location information and a reasoned explanation are sufficient.

### Submission and important notes

- **Submission:** Scan the form and send it by email to **[support@whoisnextapp.com](mailto:support@whoisnextapp.com)**. You may attach screenshots or other documents as email attachments.
- After reporting, it is recommended that you **block** the relevant user.
- Reports are evaluated within the framework of the Community Guidelines and Terms of Use; acknowledgment of receipt and decision notification may be applied under DSA.
