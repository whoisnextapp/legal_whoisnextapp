## 5. Right of Withdrawal Information

**General rule (Distance Contracts Regulation, Art. 9):** The consumer has the right to withdraw from the contract within **fourteen days** without giving any reason and without paying a penalty. For contracts concerning performance of services, the withdrawal period begins on **the day the contract is concluded**.

**Exceptions (digital service — Regulation, Art. 15(1)):** The right of withdrawal **cannot be used** in the following cases:

- **Art. 15(1)(ğ):** Services performed **immediately** in electronic form or **intangible goods delivered immediately** to the consumer.
- **Art. 15(1)(h):** Services **the performance of which has begun with the consumer’s consent before the end of the withdrawal period**.

Because access to WIN Premium begins immediately upon purchase and/or performance has begun with your consent, **exceptions to the right of withdrawal may apply**. This situation and the warning that “performance of the service may begin immediately” are shown on the purchase screen (see [Subscription and Purchase Terms — Section 4](/sozlesmeler/abonelik-ve-satin-alma-kosullari#section-4-purchase-process-and-establishment-of-the-contract)).

**Withdrawal notice (Art. 11):** Where your right of withdrawal exists, it is sufficient to send the notice to the provider **in writing** or by **durable medium** (e-mail, etc.).

**Address / contact to which the withdrawal notice is to be sent:** legal@whoisnextapp.com and the channel stated in the Contact section of the [Terms of Use](/sozlesmeler/kullanim-kosullari#164-contact).

**Deficient information (Art. 10):** Where you have not been informed as required regarding the right of withdrawal, the provisions on not being bound for fourteen days and (where conditions arise) a one-year period remain reserved.

## 6. Refund and Price Repayment

**Special price repayment commitment:** WIN does not undertake an **additional goodwill refund policy** such as “refund within the first X days”.

**Refund requests:** Because in-app purchases are made through the **Apple App Store** or **Google Play Store**, refund / reimbursement requests are in most cases subject to the procedures of the **relevant store** (Apple or Google). For a refund request:

- **iOS:** Apple Support / [getsupport.apple.com](https://getsupport.apple.com) and App Store subscription cancellation steps.
- **Android:** Google Play Help / [support.google.com/googleplay](https://support.google.com/googleplay) and Play subscription cancellation steps.

**Mandatory consumer rights:** Your mandatory refund and compensation rights arising from the Law and the Distance Contracts Regulation remain reserved. For defective service and optional remedies, [Subscription and Purchase Terms](/sozlesmeler/abonelik-ve-satin-alma-kosullari) Section 9 and the [Distance Sales Agreement](/sozlesmeler/mesafeli-satis-sozlesmesi) apply.

## 7. Payment Obligation Warning and Pre-Information Confirmation

**Payment obligation (Regulation, Art. 8):** Clicking a confirmation button such as “Buy”, “Subscribe” or similar states that the order **creates a payment obligation** and that you accept that obligation. **Immediately before** incurring a payment obligation, price and basic information (Regulation, Art. 6(2)) are also shown on the purchase / payment screen.

**Pre-information confirmation (Regulation, Art. 7):** In the in-app flow, you may be asked to confirm that you have read this Pre-Information Form and (before moving to the store screen) the payment obligation warning. Until confirmation is given, the contract is deemed not to have been concluded.

This Pre-Information Form is read together with the [Distance Sales Agreement](/sozlesmeler/mesafeli-satis-sozlesmesi) and the [Subscription and Purchase Terms](/sozlesmeler/abonelik-ve-satin-alma-kosullari) and enters into force together with purchase confirmation.
